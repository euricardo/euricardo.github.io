Powerful and easy to use editor.

Image Map Pro™ comes with its own feature rich web app, so you can quickly and easily create the most comprehensive selection of interactive SVG image maps, pins and custom vector shapes, and immediately export them to your website.

Polygon Shapes

Draw and edit complex polygon SVG shapes directly in the Editor. No Illustrator other programs needed.

Custom Styles

Use your own colors for the shapes and tooltips to match the design of your project.

Tooltip Position

Position the tooltip exactly where you want it or use one of the default settings - top, bottom, left or right.

Content Builder

Adding content to the tooltips is super easy with the Editor. Complex grid layouts are also supported.

Connected Shapes

Group multiple vector shapes or icons together to make them share a tooltip and trigger at the same time.
